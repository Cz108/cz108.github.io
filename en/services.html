Our Services

OFFER

Trucking

Our services cover countries across Europe, from north to south and east to west, ensuring smooth delivery of your goods to your destination. We provide fast and accurate freight delivery services with a modern fleet and efficient route planning to ensure timely deliveries. Whether your cargo is large or small, we offer flexible trucking solutions and services tailored to your needs. Our experienced drivers and logistics team can handle various delivery challenges, ensuring the safe delivery of your goods.

Port Yard

Located on the shores of the Baltic Sea, our yard is strategically positioned at the intersection of key trade routes in Europe. This makes us a vital link connecting the East and West, providing convenient global distribution channels for your goods. With modern port facilities and warehouse space equipped with advanced stacking technology and equipment, we ensure the safe and precise handling of your goods. The Polish port yard is known for its advantages in multimodal transportation, seamlessly combining different modes of transport to offer flexible and efficient cargo circulation. Our professional team has extensive stacking experience, enabling quick and accurate handling of various goods, ensuring on-time sorting and loading. Regardless of the type and size of your cargo, we can provide customized stacking and warehousing solutions to meet your specific needs.

Sea Freight (FCL/LCL)

Whether you need full-container load (FCL) or less-than-container load (LCL) freight services, we offer multiple options to meet your diverse shipping needs. We understand that each client's requirements are unique, so we provide customized FCL/LCL solutions to accommodate your specific needs. Our professional team has extensive logistics experience and can provide fast and precise handling and transportation of your goods.

Overseas Warehousing

Located in the heart of Europe and connected to major transportation arteries, our overseas warehouse offers convenient access to the European market. Our modern warehousing facilities and advanced inventory management technology ensure the safe storage and precise sorting of your goods, facilitating efficient order processing. By leveraging our warehousing and logistics infrastructure, you can reduce logistics costs and better manage inventory, achieving higher economic benefits.

Container Unloading

Our logistics expert team has extensive experience in accurately and efficiently unloading various goods to ensure damage-free delivery. Regardless of the type and size of your cargo, we can provide customized container unloading solutions to meet your needs. We employ advanced equipment and safety measures to protect your goods during the unloading process. We ensure on-time delivery to meet your delivery requirements through efficient processes and precise operations.

Warehouse Integration

We assist in integrating your multiple overseas warehouses into a unified management platform, simplifying warehousing management and reducing costs. With advanced inventory management systems, we help you accurately track inventory levels and reduce excess inventory buildup. We achieve automated order processing to ensure timely and accurate order fulfillment, enhancing customer satisfaction. Through real-time data tracking, you can stay informed about inventory status and logistics processes, making better decisions. We provide customized warehouse integration solutions based on your business needs, ensuring maximum efficiency.
